# Time Series Brush Slicer - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 12, 2025

## Introduction

The Time Series Brush Slicer is a custom visual for Microsoft Power BI designed to provide interactive time range selection and filtering capabilities. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

## Overview

The Time Series Brush Slicer custom visual is available for download from Microsoft AppSource and our GitHub repository at https://github.com/slavatrofimov/Time-Series-Brush-Slicer.

## Data Collection and Privacy

### No Data Collection
**The Time Series Brush Slicer does not collect any user data whatsoever.** This includes but is not limited to:
- No personal information
- No usage statistics
- No telemetry data
- No analytics data
- No cookies or tracking mechanisms

### No External Connections
**The visual makes zero outbound network connections.** Specifically:
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is sent to third-party services
- No external APIs are called
- No internet connectivity is required for the visual to function


## Data Processing

### Local Processing Only
The Time Series Brush Slicer processes data entirely within the Power BI environment:
- Data is processed locally within your Power BI report
- All visualizations are rendered client-side
- Time range selections and filters are applied within Power BI's data model
- No data leaves your Power BI workspace

### Data Security
Since the visual operates entirely within Power BI's secure environment and makes no external connections, your data remains subject only to Microsoft Power BI's security policies and the data governance rules you have established for your Power BI tenant.

## Microsoft AppSource

When you download the Time Series Brush Slicer from Microsoft AppSource, Microsoft may collect certain information as part of the download process. This information is collected by Microsoft, not by us. For details on Microsoft's data collection practices, please review:
- [Microsoft Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement)
- [Power BI Privacy Documentation](https://docs.microsoft.com/power-bi/admin/service-security-data-protection-overview)

We do not have access to any personal information collected by Microsoft during the download process.

## Third-Party Services

The Time Series Brush Slicer does not integrate with, connect to, or utilize any third-party services or APIs.

## Children's Privacy

The Time Series Brush Slicer does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As a Power BI custom visual, it is intended for use by business professionals within enterprise environments.
